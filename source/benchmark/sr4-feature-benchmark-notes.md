# SR4 feature benchmark notes

SR4 should eventually be tested on complete examples of:

1. strong sports news feature;
2. weak sports news feature;
3. strong profile;
4. weak profile;
5. strong issue feature;
6. weak issue feature;
7. strong explanatory feature;
8. weak explanatory feature;
9. strong narrative feature;
10. weak narrative feature;
11. good prose with thin reporting;
12. strong reporting with weak structure;
13. ethical-risk case;
14. piece that is not really a feature.

For each case, record the likely form, feature promise, main reader-journey break, selected teachable move, revision experiment, ethical caution, confidence level and acceptable alternative diagnoses.
