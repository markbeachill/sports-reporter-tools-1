Sports Reporter Tools

A small library of AI analysis and tutoring tools for sports writing — match reports and previews — together with the design notes and papers behind them.

The tools are copiable prompts: take one, paste it into an AI assistant, and give it a piece of writing to analyse. The papers and design discussion record how the tools were reasoned out and tested.

New here? Start with SR1 · Match Report Analyser, or the beginner route SR2 · Match Report Coach.
